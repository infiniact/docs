iaterm — Privacy Policy

Effective: 27 June 2026 · INFINIACT CO., LTD

Short version. iaterm is a desktop AI coding agent that runs on your computer. You bring your own LLM provider API key (BYOK). Your key, prompts, and the code/context the agent sends are transmitted directly from your machine to the LLM provider you choose — never to an INFINIACT server. iaterm has no backend and no account, and does not sell your data or run tracking by default.

1. Who we are

iaterm (“the app”) is published by INFINIACT CO., LTD (“INFINIACT”, “we”). This policy covers what the app does with your data. It does not cover the third-party LLM providers you connect (see §5).

2. Our model: bring-your-own-key, local-first

iaterm runs the AI agent locally on your device and calls the API of the LLM provider you configure, using your API key. Requests go directly from your machine to that provider. INFINIACT operates no proxy, relay, or backend that your data passes through.

3. What data the app handles, and where it goes

DataWhere it’s storedWhere it’s sent
Your provider API key Locally on your device (OS keychain / local configuration). Only to your chosen provider’s API, to authenticate requests. Never to INFINIACT.
Prompts and the files / code / command output the agent includes to perform your task On your device; session history is kept locally. To your chosen provider, as needed to carry out the task you asked for.
Files and your workspace Stay on your machine. The agent reads, writes, and runs commands locally as you direct it. Only the portions the agent sends to the provider as context for your task.

iaterm has no user account, collects no name, email, or identifier, and sends nothing to INFINIACT’s servers for this purpose.

4. You direct what the agent touches

iaterm acts on your files and runs commands in the workspace you open, under your direction and approval flow. It does not access files outside what you give it, and it does not transmit your data anywhere except to the LLM provider you chose.

5. Third-party LLM providers

Data you send is transmitted to the provider you selected, whose handling is governed by their privacy policy and terms, not ours (e.g. Anthropic, DeepSeek). Choose a provider whose data practices you are comfortable with.

6. Storage and retention

Settings and session history are stored locally on your device. They remain until you delete them or uninstall the app. We have no copy of them.

7. Analytics and tracking

iaterm does not include third-party advertising or tracking, and any optional diagnostics are off by default. We do not build user profiles and do not sell or share data with data brokers.

8. Children

iaterm is a professional developer tool and is not directed to children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
